About AmusGameStore:

AmusGameStore was founded by Shivering Game Studios after the founder, Sir O'Gorath, read about the Stop Killing Games initiative.
Our goal is to provide a way for people to actually own the games they buy and not just licenses like with most other stores.
For our customers this means that no one can revoke their ownership of the games they buy from us.
This also means that they no longer have to stay connected to the internet to play a game that they've paid money for.
